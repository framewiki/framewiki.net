# Framework [Next Gen] Event
{% include sidecar.html %}
The **Framework [Next Gen] Event** was a launch event held April 21, 2026 in San Francisco, California, during which [Framework](/framework-computer-inc) announced the [Framework Laptop 13 Pro](/products/framework-laptop-13-pro), updates to [Framework Laptop 16](/products/framework-laptop-16), the [Framework Wireless Touchpad Keyboard](/products/wireless-touchpad-keyboard), a [Framework OCuLink Dev Kit](/expansion-bay/oculink-dev-kit), and [Framework Laptop Sleeves](/products/laptop-sleeve).[^3] [^2]

It is Framework's third launch event, after the [Framework Next Level Event](/events/next-level) in 2023 and the [Framework [2nd Gen] Event](/events/2nd-gen) in 2025. 

Members of the media and [Framework partners](/partners) received invitations to attend in-person, and a limited number of seats were available for community members who could arrange their own travel on an application basis.[^1] The event was also  livestreamed to Framework's YouTube channel.[^2]
